## Project Overview

**Clínyco.IA®** is an AI platform focused on **intelligent medical and commercial lead classification**.

Its current core is not just a conversational bot. It is a system designed to read conversations, structure relevant patient data, detect real buying or treatment intent, and assign an objective commercial priority to support conversion and clinical coordination.

At its current stage, **Clínyco.IA®** already works as a **lead score classifier**. It scores each conversation on a **0–100 scale** and classifies leads as **cold, warm, or hot** using signals such as:

- BMI
- insurance profile
- contact data
- verified RUT
- identified procedure
- conversational engagement
- scheduling intent
- channel context

This scoring layer is connected to Zendesk operations, customer memory, and follow-up workflows.

## Current Capabilities

Today, the platform can:

- classify leads using conversational and clinical signals
- structure contact, insurance, procedure, and patient-related data
- maintain conversational memory and historical patient context
- sync useful data into Zendesk workflows
- support scheduling and follow-up processes
- provide a foundation for more advanced intelligence models

## Recent Progress

Recent development has focused on making the platform more reliable and operationally useful. Key improvements include:

- stronger Zendesk synchronization using the ticket requester as the canonical identity
- more reliable lead score syncing into Zendesk
- improved Medinet integration with API-first scheduling flows and better authentication support
- more resilient booking flows with safer fallbacks and fewer silent failures
- stronger conversation persistence and customer memory
- synchronization of knowledge sources from Google Sheets for easier operational updates

## Product Vision

**Clínyco.IA®** is being built as an intelligence platform for **medical sales and clinical coordination**.

The goal is not to remain a text-only chatbot, but to evolve into a system capable of:

- classifying prospects more accurately
- analyzing conversion patterns
- recommending commercial actions
- assisting human agents in real time
- turning conversations into operational decisions
